Can LLMs Write Correct TLA+ Specifications? Our New Evaluation Study
=====================================================================

We have submitted a new paper evaluating whether large language models can generate
semantically correct TLA+ specifications from natural language. We evaluated 30 LLMs
across eight families on 205 TLA+ specifications. Best semantic correctness achieved
was only 8.6%, and model size did not predict quality.

`Read the full paper details <../papers/llm-tla-evaluation.html>`__
